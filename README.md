## Installation on iPhone

1. Open Safari on your iPhone
2. Visit the app URL (provided after deployment)
3. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
4. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
5. Tap "Add" in the top right
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen!

## How to Use

### Daily Routine
1. Check off each task as you complete it
2. Take your progress photo
3. Tap "Complete Day" when all tasks are done
4. Start fresh the next day!

### Viewing Progress
- Tap "Progress" tab to see your photo transformation
- Use the slider to compare Day 1 vs Today
- Browse your photo gallery

### Calendar View
- Tap "Calendar" to see all 75 days
- Green = completed days
- Red = incomplete days
- Orange = current day

### Settings
- View your start date
- Start a new challenge
- Clear all data (careful!)
